ABOUT THE EVENT

"Aptitude is to the brain what colours are to the life" The technical aspects of life are the most pivotal necessity in shaping one's life and thoughts. The brain feed is not only sharpened but is also exposed to the practical outlook of life if we indulge in the attributes related to the technicalities and application-based accoutrements of life. Hackathon trains the mind and touches the innermost gambler hiding in you. To foster the talent and your creativity , Robolution brings forth a competition -LOCK-A-THON . It is a week long competition to set your minds rolling with the realms. With attractive prizes of more than X along with goodies and e- certificates for the winning team, the event follows the theme of -'Open innovation which includes participation from all domains and fields'. The Lockathon will consist of two rounds : a screening round and a final round. The selection will be based on the ideas which you will submit and your GitHub or LinkedIn account.
This lockathon aims to help you turn your ideas into reality. You'll have all the freedom to create a product, learn new techniques for your future, or just have fun working on a project with friends. Moreover, we'll offer you a chance to work with other mentors. You will never learn faster than you will at a hackathon. As robotics in large is a multidisciplinary branch of engineering, we will be accepting ideas from students of all branches whether circuital or non-circuital. If you haven't participated in any hackathon before and if you're looking out for the opportunity to do so, you're in the right place. We welcome all newcomers and veterans alike. You are about to witness a series of events which will enlighten as well as enhance your robotics skills.

SCHEDULE

ROUND-1

2nd August -4th August

Description

  • Participants are required to form a team of 2-4 members and submit their idea on which they would like to work.
  • They also need to point out the impact of their solution.

    • ROUND-2

    5th August -8th August

    Description

  • Teams would would be selected for the next round based upon their ideas.
  • Teams selected would be given 7 days of time to build their prototype , share a video of their demonstration and share their github link to the project.

    • ROUND-3

    9th August -13th August

    Description

  • Teams showing extra ordinary performances would be selected for the final round.
  • They would have to present their projects through online mode to a panel of judges who would grade them.
  • Top 3 performers would be selected by the judges.

    FAQs

    • 1. What is Lock-A-Thon?

      Lock-a-thon is an amazing opportunity to receive help in your project from mentors in a vast array of fields, and win prizes. It is an event which promotes new talents and sharpens existing ones.

    • 2.How much does it cost?

      Lock-A-Thon is absolutely free. Any BIT student with a will to solve and develop can participate in Lock-A-Thon.

    • 3.What if I don't know how to code?

      That is not a problem. A great way to learn how to code is by attending such events. Lock-A-Thon is an event for like-minded people where they share interests, and explore their passions, whether it is Coding, Electronics or anything else, you never know what you might pick up!!

    • 4. Who is Lock-A-Thon for?

      Lock-a-thon is only for the students of BIT Mesra who are in their sophomore year or pre-final year.

    • 5. Do you have a particular theme for the Lock-a-thon?

      No. The Lock-a-thon is open. You can use any technology to build your hacks.

    • 6. What is the criteria of selection?

      You will be judged on the quality of your idea. The idea is only a part of the judgement. The skills of your team members would play a major role in your selection.

    • 7. I don't have an original idea. Can I still participate?

      Of course. Not every idea has to be unique. Infact while pursuing your not-so-unique idea you might even lead to the improvement of existing product.

    • 8.Is the selection per member or per-team basis?

      Per-team basis.

    • 9. Are there prizes for the hack categories?

      Yes. you will be awarded with cash prizes and goodies,thanks to our sponsors!

    • 10.Do we get any certificate for participation?

      Yes

    • 11.How big a team can be?

      The maximum team size is four.
